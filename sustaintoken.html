02 · Protocol

Verifiable by Design.

SustainToken is environmental impact infrastructure — not a carbon credit marketplace. We provide the cryptographic primitives and on-chain registry for organizations to record, hash, and anchor sustainability actions with institutional-grade evidence.

Every action produces a SHA-256 fingerprint of its evidence file, a Content ID pinned to IPFS, and an immutable transaction hash on BSC — making greenwashing detectable and sustainability claims independently verifiable.
